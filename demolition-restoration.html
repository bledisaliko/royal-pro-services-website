Controlled demolition during restoration projects

When drywall, flooring, ceilings, trim or other building materials are damaged, careful removal may be needed before drying, remediation or repair work can continue.

  • Controlled demolition of damaged materials
  • Drywall and ceiling removal support
  • Flooring and baseboard removal support
  • Cleanup after water, fire or mold-related damage
  • Repair and restoration planning
  • Drywall repair and flooring repair support

Restoration after mitigation

Once the affected areas are cleaned, dried or remediated, repair work may include drywall, flooring, trim, painting and interior rebuild support. Royal Pro Services helps connect the cleanup phase with the repair phase.
