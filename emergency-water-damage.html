When to call for emergency water damage help

Call immediately when water is spreading, a ceiling is leaking, a basement is flooding, flooring is soaked, drywall is wet or you are unsure whether electrical areas are safe. Early cleanup and drying support can reduce additional damage and make restoration planning clearer.
