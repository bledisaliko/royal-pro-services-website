Cleanup support after fire, smoke and soot damage

Fire damage can create visible and hidden issues, including soot residue, smoke odours, damaged materials and water damage from fire response. Royal Pro Services can help organize cleanup and restoration steps.

Fire restoration often has multiple phases

Secure

Board-up or temporary protection may be needed for damaged openings or unsafe areas.

Clean

Soot, smoke residue, odours and damaged materials are reviewed and handled based on conditions.

Restore

Demolition, drywall, flooring and rebuild work can be planned after cleanup needs are understood.
