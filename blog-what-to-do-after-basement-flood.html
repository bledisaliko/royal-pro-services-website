What To Do After A Basement Flood

A practical guide for basement flooding, water cleanup, wet flooring, drywall damage and emergency restoration steps in Toronto and the GTA.

Call NowRequest Help

Quick answer

A flooded basement can involve standing water, wet baseboards, soaked flooring, damaged contents and hidden moisture behind walls. If safe, stop the source, avoid electrical hazards, document the damage and call for cleanup and drying support before repairs are started.

Emergency checklist

  • Stay out of unsafe electrical areas.
  • Stop the water source if it is safe.
  • Move small belongings away from water if possible.
  • Take photos for documentation.
  • Call Royal Pro Services for restoration support.
