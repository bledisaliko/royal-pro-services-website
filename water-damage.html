Water Damage Restoration in Toronto & the GTA

Water can move fast through flooring, drywall, trim, insulation and structure. Royal Pro Services helps property owners respond to leaks, flooding, burst pipes, sewer backups and wet building materials.

Fast support after leaks, flooding and wet materials

When water damage is left untreated, moisture can spread behind walls, under flooring and into hidden areas. Royal Pro Services can help identify affected areas, remove damaged materials where needed, support drying and prepare the property for repair.

  • Emergency water damage restoration
  • Flood cleanup and basement flooding support
  • Burst pipe cleanup and wet drywall removal support
  • Sewer backup cleanup coordination where applicable
  • Structural drying and drying equipment setup
  • Repair and restoration planning after mitigation

Common water damage situations

Basement flooding

Standing water, soaked flooring, damaged baseboards and moisture control after heavy rain or plumbing issues.

Burst pipes

Rapid cleanup support for wet drywall, ceilings, cabinets, insulation and flooring after pipe failures.

Sewer backup

Careful cleanup coordination for affected areas that may require added safety steps and material removal.

What happens next

The first priority is understanding the source, the affected areas and the urgency. From there, the work may involve extraction, removal of damaged materials, drying equipment, moisture checks, documentation and a repair plan.
