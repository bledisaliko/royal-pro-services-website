Common mold situations

After a leak

Mold may appear after water damage, roof leaks, plumbing issues or repeated condensation.

Attic concerns

Attic mold support may involve ventilation concerns, moisture sources and affected sheathing or insulation areas.

Visible growth

Visible mold on walls, trim or ceilings should be reviewed before disturbing affected materials.

Careful work areas

Depending on the situation, remediation support may include containment, controlled removal, surface cleaning, moisture checks and preparation for repair. The site avoids unverified certification claims until they are confirmed by the business.

Concerned about mold?

Avoid disturbing affected materials if you are unsure. Send a request with photos and location details, or call if the situation is urgent.
