What to do before help arrives

  • If safe, stop the water source or shut off the water supply.
  • Avoid electrical areas, wet ceilings and sagging materials.
  • Move small valuables away from wet areas if it is safe.
  • Take photos for documentation before cleanup begins.
  • Call Royal Pro Services for next steps and restoration support.
